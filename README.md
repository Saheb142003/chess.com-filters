# ♟️ Chess.com Global Analysis Dashboard

> **Unleash Your Regional Dominance. Analyze Your Global Performance with Precision.**

Welcome to the ultimate scouting tool for Chess.com enthusiasts. This dashboard isn't just a filter; it's a high-performance engine designed to dissect your chess history across every border, time control, and opponent.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🌍 The Global Scout (Regional Analytics)

Identify your strongest and weakest territories by mapping every opponent to their respective country.

- **Full Country Mapping**: Real-time translation of Chess.com codes into full names and flags.
- **Regional Scorecards**: See your Win/Loss/Draw record against any nation on Earth.

### 📊 Performance Deep-Dive

- **Authentic W/L/D Indicators**: Iconic color-coded square indicators (Green, Red, Gray) for instant recognition.
- **Rating Progression Tracking**: Track your rating shifts (+/-) for every match.
- **Opponent Scouting**: View opponent ratings to contextualize your performance.

### 🚄 Multi-Dimension Filtering

- **Game Type Isolation**: Switch between **Blitz**, **Bullet**, and **Rapid** instantly.
- **Combined Queries**: Search for specific opponents across different countries and game types.

### 🔍 Ultra-Fast Discovery

- **Opponent & Country Search**: Two dedicated search bars to find specific rivals or nations in milliseconds.
- **Smooth Navigation**: A fixed-sticky sidebar with independent scrolling for a premium dashboard feel.

---

## 🛠️ The Tech Engine

Built with high-performance modern web technologies:

- **Backend**: Node.js & Express with a parallel-batching engine for lightning-fast fetching.
- **Frontend**: React & Vite for an ultra-responsive user interface.
- **Design**: Pure Chess.com DNA—Dark Mode, Green Accents, and professional typography.
